Frames

Frame Basics

Frames are container elements for organizing content. Press F to create frames.

Frame Templates

When you press F to select the Frame tool, a templates modal opens automatically. Choose from pre-configured frame sizes organized by category:

  • Mini Apps: Farcaster Mini App templates (Light and Dark) with pre-built UI components and structure
  • Phone: Various iPhone and Android device sizes (iPhone 16, iPhone 16 Pro, Android Compact, etc.)
  • Tablet: iPad sizes (iPad Pro 12.9", iPad Pro 11", iPad Air, iPad Mini)
  • Desktop: Standard desktop resolutions (HD, 4K, 1440p)

Mini App templates load complete designs with pre-configured components, while other templates create empty frames with the correct dimensions for your target device or screen size.
